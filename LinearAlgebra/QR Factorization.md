For any $m \times n$ matrix $A$, with linearly independent columns:
$$
A = QR
$$
Q is
- m x n
- its columns are an orthonormal basis for Col A
R is
- n x n
- upper triangular
- positive diagonal
- $||\vec{r}_j|| = ||\vec{a}_j||\quad \quad$ where $\vec{r}$ and $\vec{a}$ are columns of R and A
