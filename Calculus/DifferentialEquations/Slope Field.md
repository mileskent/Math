A slope field is a visual representation of all solutions to a first order [[Differential Equation]]

For example.
$$
\frac{dy}{dt} = f(t, y) = \frac{1}{3} y
$$

Plot field of slopes given by $\frac{dy}{dt}$ at different $(t, y)$ points.
